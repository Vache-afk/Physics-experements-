1) Position:

\[ y = h_0 + v_0 t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2 \]

2) Velocity:

\[ v = v_0 - g t \]

3) Time to fall from height \(h_0\):

\[ t_{fall} = \sqrt{\frac{2 h_0}{g}} \]

4) Maximum height if thrown up:

\[ h_{max} = h_0 + \frac{v_0^2}{2 g} \]

Legend: \(y\) = height, \(h_0\) = initial height, \(v_0\) = initial velocity, \(g\) = gravity, \(t\) = time
