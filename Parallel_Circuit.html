💡 Parallel Circuit (Drag & Add Resistors)

Drag resistors onto a branch wire to activate them (ON). Multiple resistors on the same branch are treated as series: \[ R_{\text{branch}} = \sum R \] Branches are in parallel: \[ I_{\text{branch}} = \frac{V}{R_{\text{branch}}}, \quad I_{\text{total}}=\sum I_{\text{branch}}, \quad R_{\text{total}}=\frac{V}{I_{\text{total}}} \] Therefore: more resistors connected → \(R_{\text{branch}}\) increases → current decreases → charges move slower.
